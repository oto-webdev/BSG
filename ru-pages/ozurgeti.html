Since 2011, the West Georgia production base of LLC "Black Sea Group" has been located on 2.4 hectares in the village of Likhauri, Ozurgeti.

Part of the territory includes a quality control and monitoring laboratory, a mechanization workshop, a diesel filling station, scales, an administrative part of the production - a camp, where more than 50 people live permanently. There is also a canteen, where employees are provided with three meals a day. Depending on the scale of the projects, the base can accommodate up to 80 people.

Installed on the base:

1. Ukrainian-type asphalt plant KDM-201 (2008 model) Capacity 60t/h. Produces:

§ Hot asphalt concrete with adhesive additive;

§ Hot asphalt concrete with the addition of colored pigment (colored asphalt);

2. Stationary stone crushing plants: namely, a Turkish-made SEMAK vertical rotor (50m3/h) and a screen-seconder (70m3/h)

3. Emulsion machine;
