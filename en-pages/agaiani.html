The company's goal is to introduce into production the most modern, operational and universally adapted capabilities for arranging road infrastructure. They will improve the quality of construction, reduce the cost of manufactured products, extend the road exploitation period, and the work schedule will become much more optimal

The company's main production facility is located on the 42nd kilometer of the Tbilisi-Senaki-Leselidze highway, in the village of Aghaiani, Kaspi district. The area was selected due to the presence of a large amount of inert material in the Ksani River valley. The Aghaiani base serves eastern Georgia and the capital. For western Georgia, such a base is located in the Ozurgeti district. If necessary, mobile production bases are specially created.

(At this stage, in Tianeti, Poti and Zestaponi).

Several factories and workshops are located at the Aghayan production base.
