In the modern era, any new idea and project begins with implementation. We build roads, connect cities, streets, buildings, villages. We are “Black Sea Group Company”, which was founded in 2005 and is currently the leader in the Georgian market. Immediately after its establishment, “ Black Sea Group Company” set the main goal of restoring and improving the depreciated road infrastructure in the country and throughout its existence it has implemented the most important projects for the country throughout Georgia.

"Black Sea Group" has mobile industrial and technological bases equipped with modern technologies, which, if necessary, can be mobilized in any region of Georgia: namely, asphalt plants, stationary and mobile stone crushing plants, concrete and mobile concrete plants, a concrete structure factory, a concrete products, paving stones and curbs workshop, a spiral pipe workshop, a quality control laboratory equipped with modern technologies. The company pays great attention to creating comfortable conditions for the work and rest of the personnel. Well-equipped residential and office camps and catering facilities are functioning. The company has a technical personnel training and education base, where the company's employees and novice specialists master various specialties.

"Black Sea Group" is also quite successful in the field of winemaking. In particular, under the auspices of the company "Black Sea Wine", it produces the "Saperavi" and "Tsinandali" branded wines.

In addition, the Black Sea Group Company, through Black Sea Art, sponsors the functioning of the Georgian Fine Arts Center and promotes the development of fine arts in Georgia. In particular, the Black Sea Group Company sponsored the publication of catalogs of works by famous Georgian jumpers Khita Kutateladze, Dilbo, Dimitri Eristavi and Levan Kharanauli.
