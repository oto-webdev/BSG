Aghayan Quality Testing and Materials Testing Laboratory

Since its establishment, the first priority of LLC "Black Sea Group" has been the production of products in accordance with modern standards and the highest quality of work. Therefore, the main attention of the company's management has been devoted to the smooth activities of the quality testing and materials testing laboratory.

The company's main laboratory is located in the village of Aghaiani, Kaspi district. The equipment in the building, which is located on an area of ​​200 sq.m, is arranged thematically in accordance with the order of work performed.

The division of the laboratory into departments was based on the priorities of the activities. In the testing department, it is possible to

  • Testing of inert materials;
  • Inspection of cement-concrete products;
  • Testing of asphalt-concrete mixture;
  • Preparation and development of cement concrete and asphalt concrete mix designs (recipes);
  • Bitumen quality test;
  • Laboratory research of soils.

The department of testing of released products and paved surfaces carries out

  • Quality performance of works specified in the project specifications and compliance with relevant standards in field conditions;
  • Control of the construction of road pavements made of soil and inert materials;
  • Determination of the compaction quality and bearing capacity of the road subgrade and base layer in situ;
  • Checking the longitudinal and transverse slopes of the road surface;
  • Determining the slope of the land slope and the slope of the cut;
  • Determining the straightness and undulation of the road surface;
  • Determination of the density, porosity and compaction degree of asphalt concrete pavement.

The Quality Testing and Materials Testing Laboratory can carry out the above-mentioned laboratory studies and tests in accordance with the internationally approved EN, BS, ASTM, GOST norms and standards.
